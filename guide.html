How to Verify freecash-app.com with Meta (Facebook)

Use this guide to verify your domain in Meta Business Suite so your Pixel and Events Manager work correctly for freecash-app.com.

1. Open domain settings in Meta

  1. Go to Meta Business Suite: business.facebook.com — log in.
  2. Click the gear icon (Settings) → Business settings.
  3. In the left menu, open Brand Safety → Domains (or Accounts → Domains, depending on your menu).
  4. Click Add → Add a domain.
  5. Enter: freecash-app.com (no https:// or www).
  6. Click Add and then Verify domain.

Meta will show you two verification options. Use one of them below.

2. Option A: Verify with a DNS TXT record (recommended)

  1. In Meta, choose “Verify using DNS” (or TXT record).
  2. Meta shows a TXT record you must add (e.g. facebook-domain-verification=xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx).
  3. In your domain/DNS provider (where you manage freecash-app.com — e.g. Cloudflare, Namecheap, GoDaddy):
    • Add a TXT record.
    • Host / Name: @ or freecash-app.com (or leave blank if that’s how your host means “root domain”).
    • Value / Content: paste the exact value Meta gave you (e.g. facebook-domain-verification=…).
    • Save.
  4. Wait a few minutes to 72 hours for DNS to update.
  5. Back in Meta, click Verify.

3. Option B: Verify with an HTML file

  1. In Meta, choose “Verify using HTML file”.
  2. Download the verification file Meta gives you (e.g. facebook-domain-verification-xxxxx.html).
  3. Upload that file to the root of your website so it’s available at:
    https://freecash-app.com/facebook-domain-verification-xxxxx.html
  4. No redirects: the URL must return that file with 200 and the exact contents Meta provided.
  5. In Meta, click Verify.
If your site is static (e.g. this repo), put the downloaded file in the root of what you deploy (same level as index.html), then redeploy.

4. After verification

  • The domain will show as Verified in Business settings → Domains.
  • In Events Manager, your Pixel can be associated with freecash-app.com.
  • Under Aggregated Event Measurement / Configure Web Events, you can assign the verified domain to your Pixel and choose which events to send.
