Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2025

Playful Rewards (“we,” “our,” or “us”) operates the website playfulrewards-app.com and related services (the “Service”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information that you provide directly to us and information that is collected automatically when you use our Service.

  • Account and profile information: When you sign up or use our app, we may collect your name, email address, and other contact or profile details you provide.
  • Survey and quiz responses: We collect your responses to surveys and eligibility questions (e.g., operating system, interests) to personalize offers and improve our Service.
  • Payment and payout information: To process rewards and cashouts (e.g., via PayPal), we may collect payment-related information as needed to complete transactions.
  • Device and usage data: We may collect device type, operating system, IP address, browser type, and how you interact with our website and app (e.g., pages visited, clicks).
  • Cookies and similar technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences, analyze traffic, and support advertising and tracking (e.g., for offer attribution).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service (including rewards, offers, and cashouts).
  • Process your participation in offers, surveys, and promotions.
  • Verify eligibility and prevent fraud or abuse.
  • Send you service-related communications, support replies, and (with your consent) marketing.
  • Analyze usage and trends to improve our offerings and user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

3. Sharing of Information

We may share your information with:

  • Offer and partner networks: When you complete offers (e.g., app installs, surveys), we may share relevant data with our partners and tracking systems (e.g., track.free-cash.app) for attribution and payouts.
  • Payment processors: To process cashouts (e.g., PayPal), we share necessary payment-related information with those providers.
  • Service providers: We may use third-party services for hosting, analytics, email, and support; they may process data on our behalf under contractual obligations.
  • Legal and safety: We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the public.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for functionality, analytics, and advertising. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect how the Service works (e.g., offer tracking and rewards).

5. Data Retention and Security

We retain your information for as long as needed to provide the Service, process payouts, comply with law, and resolve disputes. We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data; no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict use of your personal data, or to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights or ask questions, contact us using the details below. You may also opt out of marketing emails via the link in those emails.

7. Children

Our Service is not intended for users under the age of 13 (or higher where required). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We take steps to ensure that such transfers are subject to appropriate safeguards where required by law.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised policy on this page and update the “Last updated” date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
